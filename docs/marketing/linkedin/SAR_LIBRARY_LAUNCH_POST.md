## Draft (Play A — Pre-Launch)

BSA Officers at community banks are drafting SAR narratives from scratch — every time.

There's no shortage of SAR filing guidance. There is a shortage of narrative templates that reflect how money actually moves in MDI communities.

A cash-primary deposit from a South Side Chicago church looks different from a wire transfer at a suburban commercial bank. A community-based rotating savings group looks different from a hedge fund. But most BSA software was built for the latter — and the false positive burden falls on the BSA Officer who has to explain the difference in writing.

We're building a free SAR Narrative Library — searchable templates by pattern type, calibrated for the transaction patterns common at minority depository institutions and CDFI banks.

Launching soon. If you want early access, drop your email in the comments or send me a message.
